Welcome to Samabesi Nepal

Samabesi Nepal was founded with the goal of creating a just and equitable society in which every individual, regardless of background or circumstance, has access to the resources and opportunities necessary for success. Our mission is to transform the lives of the poor and marginalized by providing high-quality modern education, health care, social inclusion, and empowerment. We work to address and eradicate issues such as illiteracy, child labor, poverty, social inequality, health problems, and food scarcity in vulnerable communities, including people with disabilities, women, children, tribals, dalits, and other marginalized groups. Since its inception, we have been working directly with these disadvantaged groups, providing effective emergency response and disaster risk management, fostering sustainable livelihoods, and promoting peace.

Become a Volunteer

Make a Difference with Samabesi Nepal

At Samabesi Nepal, our volunteers are the heart and soul of our organization. By joining our volunteer team, you have the opportunity to make a profound impact on the lives of marginalized and underserved communities. Whether you're teaching a class, assisting in a health camp, or helping with disaster relief efforts, your time and skills can create real, positive change.

  • Contribute directly to initiatives that uplift and empower marginalized groups through education, healthcare, and social inclusion.
  • Develop new skills, gain valuable experience, and enhance your resume by working with a diverse and dynamic team.
  • Meet like-minded individuals who are passionate about making a difference and build lasting friendships and professional connections.
  • See firsthand the positive changes your efforts bring to the lives of those in need.
  • Joining is tottaly free. We don't need any money from you.
