Make a Difference with Samabesi Nepal

At Samabesi Nepal, our volunteers are the heart and soul of our organization. By joining our volunteer team, you have the opportunity to make a profound impact on the lives of marginalized and underserved communities. Whether you're teaching a class, assisting in a health camp, or helping with disaster relief efforts, your time and skills can create real, positive change.

Why volunteer with us?

Be a Catalyst for Change

01.

Empower Communities

Contribute directly to initiatives that uplift and empower marginalized groups through education, healthcare, and social inclusion.

02.

Gain Experience

Develop new skills, gain valuable experience, and enhance your resume by working with a diverse and dynamic team.

03.

Make an Impact

See firsthand the positive changes your efforts bring to the lives of those in need.
