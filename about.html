About Us

Committed to Empowering Marginalized Communities through Education, Health, and Inclusion.

Welcome to Samabesi Nepal

Samabesi Nepal was founded with the goal of creating a just and equitable society in which every individual, regardless of background or circumstance, has access to the resources and opportunities necessary for success. Our mission is to transform the lives of the poor and marginalized by providing high-quality modern education, health care, social inclusion, and empowerment. We work to address and eradicate issues such as illiteracy, child labor, poverty, social inequality, health problems, and food scarcity in vulnerable communities, including people with disabilities, women, children, tribals, dalits, and other marginalized groups. Since its inception, we have been working directly with these disadvantaged groups, providing effective emergency response and disaster risk management, fostering sustainable livelihoods, and promoting peace.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a just and equitable society where all individuals, regardless of their background or circumstances, have access to the resources and opportunities they need to thrive. We envision a world where human rights, gender equality, education, and development are protected and promoted through the active involvement of society. By empowering marginalized populations through capacity building training, income generation activities, employment opportunities, and livelihood support, we aspire to build a future where every person can lead a life of dignity and self-sufficiency.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring transformative changes in the lives of poor and marginalized people through the provision of quality modern education, health services, social inclusion, and empowerment. We aim to address and eradicate issues such as illiteracy, child labor, poverty, social inequality, health problems, and food shortages among vulnerable communities, including persons with disabilities, women, children, tribals, dalits, and other marginalized sections of society. By working directly with these disadvantaged groups, we strive to deliver effective emergency response and disaster risk management, foster sustainable livelihoods, and promote peace, reconciliation, and human rights.

Our Team

Our team is composed of dedicated professionals and passionate volunteers who work tirelessly to bring our vision to life. From educators and health professionals to social workers and community organizers, each member of Samabesi Nepal plays a crucial role in driving our mission forward.
